## Photo Sources

### Free & Legal Sources:

1. **Wikimedia Commons** (https://commons.wikimedia.org)
   - Search: "London Underground [station name]"
   - Filter by license: Public Domain or CC-BY-SA
   - High-quality photos from contributors

2. **Unsplash** (https://unsplash.com)
   - Search: "London Underground" or "London tube station"
   - All photos are free to use
   - Credit photographers when possible

3. **Pexels** (https://www.pexels.com)
   - Search: "London Underground"
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required

4. **Flickr Creative Commons** (https://www.flickr.com/creativecommons/)
   - Search: "London Underground [station name]"
   - Filter by CC licenses
   - Check specific license requirements

5. **Transport for London (TfL) Open Data**
   - Some imagery available under Open Government License
   - Check: https://tfl.gov.uk/corporate/terms-and-conditions/transport-data-service

## Photo Guidelines

- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Orientation**: Landscape preferred
- **Content**: Should show:
  - Platform areas (for train, signal, platform door assets)
  - Concourse/ticket halls (for escalators, lifts, ticket machines)
  - Infrastructure (for CCTV, ventilation, power supply)
  - Clear view of station environment

- Generic concourse/ticket hall images
- Generic tunnel/infrastructure images

These are currently sourced from Unsplash as placeholders.
